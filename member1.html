Module Objective

The main objective of the Driver Management module is to efficiently manage and organize all driver-related activities within the system. This module aims to streamline driver registration, monitor their shifts, assign duties effectively, and evaluate performance to ensure smooth operations.

It helps the organization maintain proper records, improve coordination between drivers and management, and enhance productivity and service quality.

About Module 1 - Driver Management

The Driver Management module is designed to handle all driver-related operations in the system. It facilitates driver registration, shift tracking, duty assignment, and performance monitoring.

Key Functionalities:

  • Driver registration and profile management
  • Tracking driver shifts and availability
  • Managing duty assignments
  • Monitoring driver performance
  • Maintaining driver records for operational efficiency

This module ensures effective coordination between drivers and the organization, improving productivity, accountability, and service quality within the system.
